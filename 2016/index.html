Virtual Machine Meetup

Call for Participation at the VMM2016

The 3rd Virtual Machine Meetup (VMM '16) is a venue for discussing the latest research and developments in the area of managed language execution. It will be held on 1st and 2nd of September at the Università della Svizzera italiana (USI), Lugano, Switzerland and is part of the Managed Languages & Runtimes Week 2016 (http://manlang16.inf.usi.ch/, other colocated events are PPPJ '16 and JTRES '16, room Auditorium from 9am - 5pm).
